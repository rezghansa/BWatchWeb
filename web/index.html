The Black Watch Story

ABOUT US

Talented Recurruimenet & Talent Aquisition.

Blackwatch International. was established July 09, 2015, with the sole aim of providing lucrative employment abroad for our Sri Lankan young men and women, who could not find placements in their motherland itself, due to the lack of sufficient job- opportunities, even to those who possessed the required qualifications!
The Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment, and the North Western Provincial Council have been giving their unstinting support to Blackwatch International in their endeavour in finding job opportunities and placements in foreign countries for our youngsters, to enable them progress and prosper in their lives.
Blackwatch International takes pride in stating: It has won the trust and the confidence of the Foreign Agents it is associated with, by supplying them only with the most suitable personnel for specified positions, who are able and capable of performing the duties entrusted them, to the fullest satisfaction and approval of their employers, abroad!
It gives Blackwatch International the utmost pleasure and satisfaction to observe those who they had sent abroad, leading prosperous lives, after their return to Sri Lanka.

Meet our board of officials

We Achived

We have already won an award from Government of Sri Lanka, for reaching the top level in supplying foreign employment to Sri Lankan.
The government of Sri Lanka has empowered Greenway Agencies by offering A government training center for ladies, who seek employment as housekeepers in foreign countries.
Blackwatch International has made contacts with some reputed manpower agents in Cyprus, Singapore, the Middle East Countries, etc, with the sole aim of finding job- opportunities for the unemployed, in Sri Lanka.
We are happy, proud and contended, the personnel we had sent abroad have earned sufficiently, to enable them to have homes of their own, and possess enough money to invest well for their livelihood, on their return to Sri Lanka. We consider every one we send abroad, as a member from our own family circles
